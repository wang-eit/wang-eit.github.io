    Welcome to Wang Research Group at EIT Ningbo

                                    – a hub of innovation in the field of energy storage


        Our passionate team of electrochemists and material scientists is fully committed to advancing energy storage technologies, with an emphasis on all-solid-state Li/Na batteries. By leveraging the principles of chemistry, materials science, and electrochemistry, we're addressing key challenges related to the core materials, interfaces, and engineering of all-solid-state batteries. Join us on this exciting journey to reshape the future of energy storage and be a part of pioneering solutions for sustainable energy storage.
